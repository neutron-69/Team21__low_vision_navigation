Topic - 15
Project Title: Vision–Language Models for Low-Vision Navigation Assistance

Problem Statement:
Visually impaired users need reliable spatial descriptions rather than generic captions. This project explores whether VLMs can generate actionable, spatially grounded descriptions of real-world scenes, especially cluttered Indian street environments.

Expected Research Outcome:
A navigation-oriented captioning system that prioritizes hazards and spatial relations. 
Research Angle: Spatial reliability metrics for captions. 
Demo: Camera image → Spoken navigation cues.
